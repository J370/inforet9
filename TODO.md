# Topic Choosing

## List of potential topics

- Cryptocurrencies (e.g., Bitcoin): abundant posts on Twitter/X, Reddit, news sites - good for opinion balance.
- Electric Vehicles (e.g., Tesla): lots of forum, Reddit, and Twitter discussion; product reviews for sentiment.
- Large Language Models / ChatGPT: active discussions on Twitter, Reddit, Hacker News - rich opinions and rapid evolution.
- Climate Change & Policy: news, blog posts, and social media; useful for polarized opinions and timeline analysis.
- Streaming Services (Netflix, Disney+): many user reviews and tweets; easy to collect ratings + text.
- Gig Economy Platforms (Uber, Deliveroo): driver and rider/customer opinions on forums and Twitter.
- Mental Health Apps (Calm, Headspace): app store reviews + tweets; balance may vary but useful for user-experience analysis.
- Fast Fashion vs Sustainable Fashion: blogs, Instagram comments, and Twitter - good for value/ethical opinion splits.
- Vaccines & Public Health (historical COVID topics): large volume but sensitive; ensure ethical handling and balanced sources.
- Higher Education Platforms (Coursera, edX): course reviews and Reddit discussions; good for quality/usability sentiment.
- Renewable Energy Technologies (solar, wind): industry news, forums, local council comments - useful for geo/time analyses.

## Vote

- Smartphone Brands (Apple vs Samsung): reviews, forums, and social posts - good for aspect-based sentiment (battery, camera).
- Video Games / Consoles (PlayStation vs Xbox): forums, Reddit, Steam reviews - plenty of opinionated text.

## Final

- Food decision making: restaurant reviews, food blogs, social media posts about meals - good for sentiment and aspect-based analysis (taste, service, price).
  - Plant-Based Foods (e.g., Beyond Meat): product reviews, forums, and Reddit for taste/ethics sentiment.
